About the Finance Tracker

The Finance Tracker project was designed as a school assignment to demonstrate web development skills.

It includes multiple HTML pages, CSS styling, navigation, lists, tables, images, and more.

Our goal is to merge technology and learning, giving students hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, and finance concepts.

Fun fact: This site passes W3C validation with no errors!
